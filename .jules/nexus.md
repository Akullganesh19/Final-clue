## 2025-07-01 — Crime Series Detection
**Product understood as:** A case-linkage and evidence-triage system for cold cases that establishes confidence-based linkages between isolated crimes.
**Derivation reasoning:** The product has `Case` and `Linkage` entities. Users (investigators) examine pairwise linkages between cases to identify patterns. However, if a perpetrator commits a string of 5 crimes, the investigator currently has to manually trace A->B, B->C, C->D, D->E to realize it's a single series. The data clearly supports grouping these pairwise linkages into larger connected components (clusters) that represent an entire "crime series," automatically extracting the shared MO, core entities, and overall timespan across all cases in the series.
**Feature built:** Added `detectCrimeSeries` in `src/utils/seriesDetection.ts` which uses graph traversal (BFS) to identify connected components of cases that share high-confidence linkages, aggregating their shared entities, MO categories, and date ranges into a unified `CrimeSeries` object.
**User impact:** Investigators can now immediately view automatically identified crime series (clusters of 2+ cases) along with their defining shared characteristics (vehicles, MOs, time spans), dramatically reducing the manual effort needed to spot a serial offender's pattern.
**Next logical feature:** Automated series naming or a timeline visualization for each detected crime series.
