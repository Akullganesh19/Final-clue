## 2024-03-24 — Next-Case Predictive Prefetching
**Product understood as:** A case-linkage and evidence-triage system for cold cases. Investigators heavily explore connections between similar cases.
**Prediction invented:** A Next-Case Prediction Engine (`OraclePredictor`) that anticipates which cases an investigator will likely examine next based on the case they are currently viewing, and automatically prefetches them in the background.
**Data used:** The signal used is entity overlap (specifically shared weapons and vehicles) and shared Modus Operandi (MO) categories.
**Impact:** Investigators experience near-zero perceived load times when navigating between strongly linked cases, making the app feel impossibly ahead of their thought process.
**Next opportunity:** Predicting likely evidence search queries based on the investigator's recent sequence of reviewed case entities.
